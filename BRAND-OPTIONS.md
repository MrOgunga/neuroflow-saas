# Neuroflow Brand Options

## Current Best Description

Neuroflow is a gentle ADHD support app that helps people restart, track small wins, and stay grounded without the pressure of a traditional productivity tool.

## Strong Name Options

### 1. Neuroflow

- calm
- credible
- already fits the product
- strongest if you want something soft and app-like

### 2. Resetly

- easy to remember
- clear emotional promise
- very strong for ADHD positioning
- feels friendly and modern

### 3. Return

- emotionally powerful
- matches the real value of the product
- short and premium
- may need branding help because it is broad

### 4. Steady

- simple
- reassuring
- easy to explain
- less unique, but very human

### 5. Grounded

- feels emotionally safe
- speaks to overwhelm relief
- more wellness-leaning than tracker-leaning

### 6. Restart

- very clear value
- memorable
- strong for viral messaging
- broad word, so trademark and domain checks matter

### 7. Looplight

- more brandable and original
- suggests progress without pressure
- creative, but needs a little explanation

### 8. Driftback

- built around returning after slipping
- emotionally true for ADHD users
- more niche and poetic

## My Top Three

1. Neuroflow
2. Resetly
3. Return

## Best Positioning Lines

### Option A

The reset-friendly tracker for real ADHD days.

### Option B

An ADHD support app for restarting, tracking small wins, and staying grounded.

### Option C

A gentle progress app for people who need help returning, not more pressure.

## Recommendation

If you want:

- calm and credible: `Neuroflow`
- catchy and more viral: `Resetly`
- emotionally powerful and premium: `Return`
